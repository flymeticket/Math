MYP Criterion A & C Sample Page

This sample shows how a student can combine accurate mathematical understanding with clear communication.

Task

A rectangle has a length of 3x + 5 and a width of x - 2. Write an expression for the area and simplify it.

Criterion A Response

Area = length x width

Area = (3x + 5)(x - 2)

Area = 3x2 - 6x + 5x - 10

Area = 3x2 - x - 10

Criterion C Communication Checks

  • Formula is stated before substitution.
  • Brackets are used correctly.
  • Expansion steps are shown in order.
  • The final answer is simplified and clearly labelled.
