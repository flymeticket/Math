MYP Maths Command Terms

Command terms tell students what kind of thinking and response the IB task expects. In MYP Maths, marks are often lost because a student knows the topic but responds at the wrong depth.

Strategy: underline the command term first, then decide whether the answer needs only a result, working, a reasoned explanation, or an evaluation of the method.
Command Term What It Means Student Response Checklist
State Give a specific answer without extended working. Use correct units and notation.
Calculate Find a numerical answer. Show enough working for the method to be clear.
Verify Confirm that a statement or result is true. Substitute, test, or use a second method.
Justify Give valid mathematical reasons for a decision. Connect each step to a rule, property, or result.
Explain Make the reasoning clear in words and mathematics. Use sentences, diagrams, and notation together.
Evaluate Judge the strengths, limits, and accuracy of a method or model. Discuss assumptions, reliability, and real-life meaning.
