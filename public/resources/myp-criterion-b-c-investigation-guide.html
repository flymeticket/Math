The Secret to Cracking Criterion B

A step-by-step guide to MYP Math investigations with Criterion C communication built in.

Step 1

Collect and Organize Data

Create a clear table with labels, units, and enough rows to reveal a pattern.

Step 2

Look for Differences or Ratios

Test whether the pattern is linear, quadratic, exponential, or another relationship.

Step 3

Generalize the Rule

Write a formula in words first, then express it using algebraic notation.

Step 4

Verify the Formula

Substitute values from the original table and test an unseen value.

Step 5

Communicate Clearly

Use headings, tables, graphs, notation, and explanation so another student can follow the investigation.
